Saída Certa

Mobile solution for clients to pay their nightclub tab.

Problem

Big lines to pay your tab on nightclubs are boring and make people leave earlier.

Solution

Using Saída Certa you have real time access to you tab and can check what you have asked so far.

If you want to leave you can pay your tab directly from your phone and head towards the exit.
