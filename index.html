Saída Certa

Mobile payment solution for nightclub goers to pay their tab and leave without waiting in line.

Problem

In Brazil, you receive a tab card whenever you go to a nightclub.

Big lines to pay your tab are boring and a waste of time.

People leave early to avoid lines and clubs lose profit.

Solution

Using Saída Certa you have real time access to your tab and are able to check their consumption.

If you want to leave, pay your tab directly from your phone and your're free to go.
